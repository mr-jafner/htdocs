Tools & Gadgets

  • Acmer S1 Laser Engraver - I've been having a lot of fun with this, mainly for engraving and cutting wood for projects
    • I also have their honeycomb bed, which is great for the material and whatever surface you have behind it
    • This does benefit from using some software:
      • Inkscape - free vector graphics software, great for designing things to cut/engrave
      • Lightburn - popular software to control the laser engraver itself... I'm currently using the free Acmer software, but may upgrade
      • LaserGRBL - another free alternative for controlling laser engravers
      • Boxes.py - an online tool for generating laser-cut boxes parametrically
  • Soundcore A40 Earbuds - reasonably priced earbuds with decent sound and active noise cancelling
